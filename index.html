About Us


We are The Kingdom


The Kingdom is a non-profit organization dedicated to saving and protecting various animal species from extinction.

The Kingdom was founded in 2015 by a group of concerned individuals who cared deeply for the wildlife.


We Care


Over the years our Organization has rescued 1300+ animals in the US, more than 2000 animals in the European states and over 3000 in Asia.

These creatures can't speak for themselves, but they need help. Join us in this initiative and let's make Earth a safe place for them.

Contribute to our cause

At The Kingdom, we are dedicated to protecting and preserving the world's precious animal species. Our mission is to ensure a brighter, more sustainable future for these incredible creatures. We can't do it alone; we need your support to make a real impact.

Ways You Can Contribute:

  • Donate: Your financial support goes a long way in funding vital conservation efforts and wildlife rescue missions.
  • Volunteer: Join our dedicated team of volunteers, and get involved in hands-on conservation activities and awareness campaigns.
  • Spread Awareness: Use your voice to educate others about the importance of wildlife conservation. Share our message on social media, and encourage others to get involved.

Every contribution, no matter how big or small, helps us protect these remarkable creatures and their habitats. Together, we can make a difference for the planet and the animals that call it home.
